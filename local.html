The City of Win

We’d be lying if we didn't mention that part of the reason for getting married was to bring all of our family and friends to the city we love and call home. There are many, many things to do in Chicago—and a weekend will barely scrape the surface. But, here are a few of our favorites.



Attractive Attractions

The Lakefront Trail

Walk. Bike. Get some exercise while you get to know the city.


The Art Institute of Chicago

Don’t forget to check out the Thorne Miniatures in the museum’s lower level—you’ll see why it’s our favorite!


Millenium Park

Go see ’Da Bean and wriggle through a plethora of fountains, amusements, and trails.


The Museum of Science and Industry

Science. Industry. It’s a fascinating place if you like either of those things.


The Robie House

You can take a tour of this Frank Lloyd Wright masterpiece. Hot damn!
